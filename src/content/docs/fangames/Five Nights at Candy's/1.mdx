Five Nights at Candy's is a indie point-and-click survival horror video game developed and published by Emil Macko and is the first installment of the Five Nights at Candy's series.

No company is a company without a rival. Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria has closed for good. And so, another restaurant it's going to take it's place on the world of entertainment. What could go wrong?
